PDF logbook export

Exported in English for international submission. Includes the flight ledger plus by-type and simulator summaries and a certification block. EASA is the ICAO-standard European layout; GCAA (UAE) keeps the FLIGHT/PICUS columns, adds an FSTD-session block and a licence-total reconciliation note.

Roster import

Paste the monthly roster JSON to pre-load planned flights (operating legs only; deadhead and off/standby excluded). After each flight, open it and tick “Mark as flown” to record actual times. Planned flights are excluded from totals, currency and PDF until flown.
